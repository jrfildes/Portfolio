Accommodation

We have a number of options available for those who would like to stay overnight.

Dongers

From $90 per night

Located right on the border fence to South Australia, we have 11 'Donger' rooms, ranging from Single Beds, to Doubles, to a combination of the two for larger groups. Bookings are essential to avoid disappointment: sleeping in your car or behind the bar

Unpowered Sites

$25 per car

For those of you with Camper Trailers, Caravans, or even if you've just a swag but a bit of a snowflake in terms of personal hygiene, $25 will get you a site with access to our dedicated camping toilets, shower facilities and a camp kitchen, with lit pathways to guide you to and from the pub at night.

Powered Sites

$35 per car

If you wish to enjoy the luxury of plug in power with your camping setup, throw $35 a night per car our way and you can claim one of four powered sites just behind the accommodation block.

Bush Camping

$10 per car

If you are after the authentic outback travelling experience, our bush camping option offers a simple site to set up your swag, tents (ground or rooftop), for only $10. You're not far away from the pub toilets, but if you want to avail yourself of the dust and dirt a bore water shower will set you back a crisp $5 note per person.
