Visiting Us

As common knowledge would indicate, we are the connecting point for three states: Queensland, New South Wales, and South Australia; so naturally there are three main points of entry, where conditions can vary.

Below you will find a number of handy links and quick pointers based on where you will be coming from. For all routes, it is best to contact us before you leave the last point of cell signal, so we know when to expect you or send out a search party...

Weather

One of the main barriers to entry in our part of the Outback is water. If it rains more than 5-1mm on any section of any dirt road coming into the Corner Store, consider that road closed.
